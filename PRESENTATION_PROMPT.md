# Presentation Creation Prompt

**Role:** Expert Presentation Designer & Brand Strategist
**Topic:** Galagama Gems & Jewellery - Website & Brand Overview
**Goal:** Create a compelling, visually stunning slide deck presentation that showcases the Galagama Gems website, its unique features, and the brand's heritage.

---

## Context & Source Material

**Brand Identity:**
*   **Name:** Galagama Gem & Jewellery
*   **Tagline:** "Where tradition meets innovation in fine jewelry craftsmanship"
*   **Heritage:** Over 50 years of legacy, based in Pabahinna, Sri Lanka.
*   **Mission:** Bridging the gap between dreams and reality through custom design and expert craftsmanship.
*   **Values:** Master Craftsmanship, Collaborative Creation ("Your Vision, Our Art"), Premium Materials, Lifetime Partnership.

**Key Website Features:**
1.  **Immersive Visuals:**
    *   Parallax scrolling effects.
    *   Ken Burns effect on background images.
    *   Dark, premium aesthetic with gold accents (`#FBBC04`, `#CA8A04`).
2.  **Custom Design Platform (The "Crown Jewel" Feature):**
    *   Interactive canvas for drawing and shape manipulation.
    *   Real-time pricing estimation.
    *   Selection of metals (Gold, Platinum, Silver), Gemstones (Precious, Semi-Precious), and Settings.
    *   "Your Vision, Our Art" philosophy.
3.  **Product Showcase:**
    *   Featured collections (Rings, Necklaces, Earrings).
    *   High-quality imagery with hover effects.

---

## Presentation Structure Request

Please generate a **10-12 slide presentation outline**. For each slide, provide:
1.  **Slide Title**
2.  **Visual Description:** (Detailed instructions for imagery, layout, and style - e.g., "Split screen showing the raw gemstone vs. the final ring," or "Screenshot of the Custom Design interface with a cursor drawing a ring.")
3.  **Key Talking Points:** (Bulleted list of script notes)
4.  **Speaker Notes:** (A brief narrative sentence for the presenter)

### Required Slides:

*   **Slide 1: Title Slide** - Elegant, using the brand colors (Gold & Dark Slate).
*   **Slide 2: The Legacy** - 50+ years, Sri Lankan heritage, family workshop to renowned destination.
*   **Slide 3: The Digital Experience** - Overview of the website's premium feel, parallax effects, and user experience.
*   **Slide 4: Featured Collections** - Showcasing the "Sapphire Elegance Ring", "Ceylon Moonstone Necklace", etc.
*   **Slide 5: Innovation: Custom Design Studio** - **(Crucial Slide)** Explain the interactive design tool where users can draw and build their own jewelry.
*   **Slide 6: How It Works** - Step-by-step: Choose Metal -> Choose Gem -> Draw/Design -> Real-time Price.
*   **Slide 7: Craftsmanship & Quality** - Mention the artisans, hand-engraving, and ethical sourcing.
*   **Slide 8: Sustainability & Ethics** - Certified suppliers, fair trade, environmental responsibility.
*   **Slide 9: Customer Journey** - From "Dream" (Design) to "Reality" (Delivery), including lifetime warranty.
*   **Slide 10: Conclusion & Call to Action** - "Start your design journey today."
